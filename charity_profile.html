About Us

Founded in 2010, Hope Animal Shelter has rescued over 1,000 animals and helped them find loving homes. Our mission is to provide shelter, medical care, and rehabilitation to abandoned and abused animals while working to find them permanent homes through our adoption programs.

We rely entirely on donations and volunteer support to continue our important work in the community.
